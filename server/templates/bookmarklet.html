Install the Bookmarklet

Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar:

Content Analysis

If you don't see your bookmarks bar, press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows/Linux) or Command+Shift+B (Mac) to show it.

How to Use

  1. Navigate to any article or content-heavy webpage you want to analyze.
  2. Click the "Content Analysis" bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar.
  3. Wait a few moments while the content is analyzed.
  4. Review the key points extracted from the content in the sidebar.
  5. Click on any key point to highlight its source in the original page.
  6. Use the "Find supporting evidence" button to search for verification.
  7. Check alignment between original content and search results.

Features

  • Extract: Identify and summarize key points from the webpage.
  • Retrieve: Find supporting evidence for specific claims.
  • Compare: Check alignment between original content and external sources.
